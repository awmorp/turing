; 4-state busy beaver
; When run with blank input, prints a number of 1's then halts.
; See, eg, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Busy_beaver for background on the busy beaver problem.

0 * * * a

a _ 1 r b
a 1 1 l b
b _ 1 l a
b 1 _ l c
c _ 1 r halt
c 1 1 l d
d _ 1 r d
d 1 _ r a
